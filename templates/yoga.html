Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

Benefits: Improves posture, balance, and calm focus.

Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

Benefits: Stretches the shoulders, hamstrings, calves, arches, and hands.

Warrior I (Virabhadrasana I)

Benefits: Strengthens the legs, opens hips and chest, and stretches arms and legs.

Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II)

Benefits: Strengthens the legs and arms, improves balance and stability.

Tree Pose (Vriksasana)

Benefits: Improves balance and stability in the legs.

Bridge Pose (Setu Bandhasana)

Benefits: Stretches the chest, neck, and spine; strengthens the back, buttocks, and hamstrings.

Triangle Pose (Trikonasana)

Benefits: Stretches the legs, groin, hips, and spine; strengthens the legs and core.

Child's Pose (Balasana)

Benefits: Gently stretches the hips, thighs, and ankles; calms the brain and helps relieve stress.

Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)

Benefits: Strengthens the spine, stretches the chest, shoulders, and abdomen.

Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana)

Benefits: Stretches the spine, shoulders, and hamstrings; calms the mind.

Boat Pose (Navasana)

Benefits: Strengthens the abdomen, hip flexors, and spine.

Bow Pose (Dhanurasana)

Benefits: Stretches the entire front of the body, strengthens the back muscles.

Wheel Pose (Chakrasana)

Benefits: improves spine flexibility, strengthens the body, and energizes the mind

Fish Pose (Matsyasana)

Benefits: Stretches the deep hip flexors and muscles between the ribs; strengthens the upper back and back of the neck.

The Half Fishe Pose (Ardha Matsyendrasana)

Benefits: Increases flexibility in the spine, shoulders, and hips; stimulates digestive fire.

Headstand (Shirshasana)

Benefits: Strengthens the upper body, spine, and core; improves balance and concentration.

Shoulder Stand (Sarvangasana)

Benefits: Calms the brain, reduces stress and depression; stretches the shoulders and neck.

Corpse Pose (Shavasana)

Benefits: Relaxes the body, calms the mind, and reduces stress and tension.

Lotus Pose (Padmasana)

Benefits: Calms the brain, stimulates the pelvis, spine, abdomen, and bladder.

Cat-Cow Pose (Marjaryasana-Bitilasana)

Benefits: Stretches the back torso and neck, provides a gentle massage to the spine and belly organs.

Chair Pose (Utkatasana)

Benefits: Strengthens the thighs, calves, and spine; stretches the shoulders and chest.

Reclining Bound Angle Pose (Supta Baddha Konasana)

Benefits: Stretches the groin, thighs, and knees; stimulates the abdominal organs.

Revolved Triangle Pose (Parivrtta Trikonasana)

Benefits: Strengthens and stretches the legs, hips, and spine; opens the chest to improve breathing.

Extended Side Angle Pose (Utthita Parsvakonasana)

Benefits: Strengthens and stretches the legs, knees, and ankles; stretches the groins, spine, waist, chest, and lungs.

Half Moon Pose (Ardha Chandrasana)

Benefits: Strengthens the abdomen, ankles, thighs, buttocks, and spine; stretches the groins, hamstrings, and calves.

King Pigeon Pose (Kapotasana)

Benefits: Stretches the thighs, groins, abdomen, chest, shoulders, and neck; strengthens the back.

Locust Pose (Salabhasana)

Benefits: Strengthens the muscles of the spine, buttocks, and backs of the arms and legs; stretches the shoulders, chest, belly, and thighs.
